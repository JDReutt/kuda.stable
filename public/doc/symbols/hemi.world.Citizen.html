Class hemi.world.Citizen

A Citizen is a uniquely identifiable member of a World that is able to send Messages through the World's dispatch. The Citizen's id is all that is necessary to retrieve the Citizen from its World, regardless of its type.

  • Defined in: <js/hemi/world.js>
